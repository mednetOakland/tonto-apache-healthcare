This is Jessica Balcom, Family Nurse Practitoner

A dedicated nursing professional with over 19 years of experience. Since 2018 I have been practicing as a Family Nurse Practitioner, focusing on delivering comprehensive care to my patients. My background includes experience as an emergency medicine nurse practitioner, as well as a commitment to serving rural communities in family practice.

I believe in the importance of holistic care and focus on treating the whole person rather than just addressing symptoms. My approach involves taking the time to listen to my patients, understanding their backgrounds, and considering their individual circumstances when formulating care plans. I feel that building strong, trusting relationships with my patients is essential for effective healthcare delivery. Practicing in rural healthcare settings has further enriched my experience. I have learned to adapt to resource limitations while still providing high-quality care. It has been incredibly rewarding to serve communities that may otherwise have limited access to healthcare services. I am passionate about providing quality healthcare and making a difference in the lives of those I serve.
